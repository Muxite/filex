Types of Fruits

There are many different types of fruits available around the world. Some common categories include:

Citrus fruits like oranges, lemons, and grapefruits are rich in vitamin C. They have a tangy flavor and are often used in juices and cooking.

Berries such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries are small, sweet fruits that are packed with antioxidants. They grow on bushes and are popular in desserts.

Tropical fruits like mangoes, pineapples, and papayas are native to warm climates. They are often very sweet and colorful.

Stone fruits including peaches, plums, and cherries have a hard pit in the center. They are typically juicy and sweet when ripe.

Apples and pears are pome fruits that grow on trees. They come in many varieties and are available year-round in most places.

Each type of fruit has its own nutritional benefits and can be enjoyed in various ways.
